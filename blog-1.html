TechStars Startup Weekend Women

2nd place winner!

FLIT.ie attended the pre-natal ward at Techstars Weekend Startup Women 2024. This is a mini-accelerator(?) where bright spark entrepreneurs pitch an idea on Friday evening, and form a team of 4-7 people to shape the idea, with intense mentoring and masterclasses provided by TechStars, over the 48-hour period.

I brought my rickety MVP and concept to this group, and emerged with a well-formed FLIT business concept, clearly aligned with the SDG goals. Subjecting an idea to intense scrutiny (particularly when the founder proposes a non-profit-extracting model) meant that FLIT was not only born, it was baptised (in fire).

The weekend culminated with a competitve pitch; in a wonderful communion of minds, the judges awarded FLIT 2nd place. All that remains is to confirm FLIT via a feasibility study.

The experience and prizes opened doors for FLIT:

* One months co-working space in the Guinnesss Enterprise Centre, led to meeting with Enterprise Ireland & DCC LEO, which in turn led to meeting with The Positive Economist, Susan Hayes Culleton, and joining the Fundr community

* A ticket to Dublin Tech Summit

* Tickets to SaaStock, Nov 2024
