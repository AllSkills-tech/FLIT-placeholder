About FLit

FLITs beginning

FLIT (Financial LITeracy for women in Ireland) came into consciousness during 2023, following the founders' experience of rearing a family and making ends meet, often in challenging circumstances with multiple calls on time and money.

Who is FLIT for

FLIT is designed to help Irish women navigate resources and provide tools to optimise income and achieve financial security.

Icon
Icon

What will FLIT provide

FLIT aims to provide useful information, such as:

  • How to navigate key financial 'decision points' when living in Ireland
  • What resources are available to help with decision making?
  • How to make financial decisions with confidence.
  • Tips and resources to help manage and structure finances.

How will FLIT provide this

FLIT will include features such as:

  • Lifestage view
  • Learning modules
  • Customisable dashboard
  • Articles and links to resources and events
