Fintech - Understanding the EcoSystem

from the perspective of the Female Customer Base

FLIT.ie representatives have been busy in recent months engaging with the Tech, Fintech, and female business worlds. Good old fashioned networking and contact-building, scanning the environment for trends, influences and changes that impact women engaging with financial services. While raising awareness by wearing the team colours.. This included: FinTech Summit, May 2024 TechFoundHER Dublin Tech Summit Karren Brady Women in Tech Summit, Manchester, TechStars networking London Tech Week ESG in Finance Summit London, June 24 InvestHER summit, June 2024

I brought my rickety MVP and concept to this group, and emerged with a well-formed FLIT business concept, clearly aligned with the SDG goals. Subjecting an idea to intense scrutiny (particularly when the founder proposes a non-profit-extracting model) meant that FLIT was not only born, it was baptised (in fire).

A massive thankyou to the FLIT team of Leena Kaloinen, Wen Dai, and WanPin Cai, who shaped the marketing message and deliverables over the course of a VERY intense 48 hours, and most importantly, required me to clarify, shape and refine the core business idea.

The weekend culminated with a competitve pitch; in a wonderful communion of minds, the judges awarded FLIT 2nd place. Next step: confirm FLIT via a feasibility study.

The experience and prizes opened doors for FLIT:

* One months co-working space in the Guinness Enterprise Centre, led to meeting with Enterprise Ireland & DCC LEO, which in turn led to meeting with The Positive Economist, Susan Hayes Culleton, and joining the Fundr community

* A ticket to Dublin Tech Summit

* Tickets to SaaStock, Nov 2024
